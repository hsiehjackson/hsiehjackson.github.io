Being a director, playwright, and protagonist simultaneously for a romantic comedy.

The record video for 2017 NTUEE sophomore drama (in chinese).

NTUEE Night is one of the best show night in NTU. Every year the sophomore drama in the night will attract 300+ audience to appreciate magnificent stage scenery, fascinating plots and excellent acting performance. When preparing for the drama, we pay lots of emphasis on the scipt and always revise it again and agaig with my teamates, just debuging until no bugs. Besides scripts, our stage scenery is also the highlight of the drama. Cooperated with makers and stagehands, they helped us design technology-based and large-scaled props, which can always amaze audience.

As for the plot of our play, it is a story about an engineer who care about his research rather than the people around him. He dedicated his life to artificial emotional intelligence; however, it is an irony that he researched on emotion for robots but lost it by himself. Therefore, the conflicts between him and his girl got more and more serious, which caused the tragedy happened...
