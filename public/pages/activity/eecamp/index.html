Chair - NTUEE Summer Camp

2018 July - 2018 August

Organize 100+ staff members to hold a six-day camp for 144 high school students.

Group photo for all members in NTUEE camp.

NTUEE Camp is the most prestigious student camp in Taiwan. With 100+ staff members, we welcome 144 high school students (selected from 1000+ applications) for intensive hacking, company visiting and various group activities. Our team consisted of different sections, including instructor, activity, curriculum-design, advertising, maintenance and equipment section. My reponsibility is to make each group get together and cooperate with alumni and school administration. We spent a whole month preparing for the camp.

During the camp period, we hold lots of activities to let them get familiar with each other soon and set up a short term course to introduce knowledge about electrical engineering. In the activities, we specially organized some performance including dancing, acting and fire dancing while we also designed group games, such as escape room, RPG and monopoly. In the courses, we taught them how to design a claw machine from scratch, which they learned circuit welding and program coding. In 2018 summer, we coordinated a 6-day, 144-high school students camp event, creating unforgettable memories for them.
