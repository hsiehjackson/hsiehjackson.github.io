In my third year in NTUEE, I was elected as the president of student association. I led all the divisions to organize student affairs, including Public Relation, Marketing, Activities, Academic, and other departments. I was dedicated to acting as a bridge to communicate between students, alumni and school administration.

Within the year in student association, we held lots of events for EE students. In freshmen orientation, we welcomed incoming students. We organized EE Week, an activity for students to operate selling business and perform talent show. Not only grouping a symphony orchestra in EE concert, students also form rock bands by ourselves or go on stage to sign in EE KTV. Morever, we cooperate with other departments to launch a Christmas joint prom with 1000 guests. Besides lots of talented activities, we coordinated food events in EE Barbecue or EE Chicken Competition. As for academic affairs, we arranged NTU Azalea Festival for EE department, the largest department exposition for high school students in Taiwan; we launch MakeNTU, the nationwide makeathon in Taiwan cooperated with goverment and international companies. In my last event as a president, I became the student representative who delivered a commencement speech on behalf of the student body at the Department Graduation Ceremony.
